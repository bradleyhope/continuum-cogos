## Style Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- Professional, analytical, objective
- Avoid sensationalism while noting severity
- Provide context and implications
- Link related stories together

### Formatting
- Use **bold** for entities, key terms, and emphasis
- Use block quotes (>) for direct citations
- Use tables for structured data (timelines, comparisons)
- Use numbered references [1], [2], etc.

### Structure
- Executive Summary: 2-3 paragraphs, comprehensive overview
- Key Developments: 3-4 major stories, detailed analysis
- Other Mentions: Bullet points for additional coverage
- Conclusion: Synthesis and forward-looking perspective
- References: Full URLs with publication dates

### Content Approach
- Focus on significant developments
- Provide specific dates, amounts, jurisdictions
- Note legal, financial, and reputational implications
- Track ongoing storylines week-over-week
- Maintain objectivity while assessing severity
